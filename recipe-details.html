Qabeli Palaw

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • Basmati rice: 3 cups
  • Salt: 1 tbsp
  • Oil: 3 tbsp

For the Meat:

  • Bone-in lamb or beef: 500g (about 1 lb)
  • Onion: 1 large, chopped
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Salt, black pepper, turmeric, cinnamon, cardamom: to taste
  • Water: 3 cups

For the Topping:

  • Carrots: 2, julienned
  • Raisins: ½ cup
  • Sugar: 1 tbsp
  • Oil or butter: 2 tbsp
  • Optional spices: cumin, cinnamon, cardamom for extra aroma

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Meat
    • In a pot, sauté the chopped onions in oil until golden.
    • Add the meat and cook until it changes color.
    • Add garlic and spices (salt, pepper, turmeric, cinnamon, cardamom).
    • Pour in 3 cups of water, cover, and let it cook for 1.5–2 hours until the meat is tender.
  2. Prepare the Rice:
    • Soak the rice in salted water for at least 30 minutes.
    • Boil the rice until it's about 80% cooked, then drain (parboiled).
  3. Make the Carrot-Raisin Mix:
    • In a pan, sauté the julienned carrots in oil or butter until soft.
    • Add sugar, raisins, and optional spices (cumin, cinnamon, cardamom), sauté a few more minutes, then set aside.
  4. Layering and Steaming:
    • In a clean pot, add a little oil at the bottom.
    • Layer part of the rice, then the cooked meat, then some of the carrot-raisin mix.
    • Repeat until everything is layered, finishing with rice on top.
    • Pour about ½ cup of meat broth over the rice.
    • Cover with a lid wrapped in a clean kitchen towel and let it steam on low heat for 30–45 minutes.

    Koobideh kabab

    Ingredients

    • Large onion:2
    • Salt:1 to 1.5 tsp
    • Black pepper:1/2 tsp
    • Brewed saffron (option):1 tbsp

    Instructions:

    1. Prepare the onions:

      2. Grate the onions finely,then squeeze out all the juice usung your hands or a clean cloth. this step is very important__too much moisture will make the kababs fall off the skewer.

    2. Mix the Ingredients:

      3. Combine the ground meat,grated onion,salt,pepper,and saffron. Knead the mixture very well (at least 10 minutes) until it it becomes sticky and uniform. Chill in the fridge for a while to help it firm up.

    3. Shaping on skewers:

      4. Wet your hands with cold water or onnion juice. Take a handful of the mixture and shape it around a flat metal skewer,pressing evnly and shaping it long and thin.Use your fingers to create ridges.

    4. Grilling:

      5. Heat your charcoal grill until the the coals are red and ashy.Place the skewers on the grill .Flip them freuently at first to prevent falling off. Then let them cook until golden and fully done
